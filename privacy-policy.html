Privacy Policy

Last updated: [Date]

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Nuggets for a Cause ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you visit our website nuggetsforacause.org or make a donation.

Please read this policy carefully. If you disagree with its terms, please discontinue use of our website.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of personal information:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, mailing address.
  • Payment Information: Credit card details (processed securely through our payment provider — we do not store full card numbers).
  • Donation Information: Donation amounts, frequency, and transaction history.
  • Communications: Messages you send us through contact forms or email.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, pages visited, referring URLs, and time spent on pages.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process donations and send receipts.
  • Communicate with you about your donation, events, and our work.
  • Send fundraising updates and newsletters (if you opted in).
  • Improve our website and fundraising campaigns.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud.
  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can control cookie settings through your browser. We use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function.
  • Analytics cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site (e.g., Google Analytics).
  • Marketing cookies: Used to deliver relevant ads and measure campaign effectiveness.

You can opt out of analytics tracking by [describe opt-out mechanism, e.g., using our cookie banner or browser settings].

  • Legal authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.
